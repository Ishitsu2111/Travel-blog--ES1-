BangkokBangkok is the capital and largest city of Thailand, known for its bustling streets, vibrant nightlife, delicious food, and rich culture. Located in central Thailand, Bangkok is home to over 8 million people, making it one of the most populous cities in the world. The city is a melting pot of cultures, with a mix of traditional Thai temples and modern skyscrapers, and a vibrant street food scene that attracts visitors from all over the world. Whether you're interested in exploring the city's rich history, trying new foods, or shopping until you drop, there is something for everyone in Bangkok.
